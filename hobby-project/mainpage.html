Kurt Vonnegut Books

Kurt Vonnegut was an American writer known for his satirical and darkly humorous novels. In a career spanning over 50 years, he published 14 novels, three short story collections, five plays, and five nonfiction works, and further collections have been published after his death.

Some of his most fascinating books and some of my favourite books are:

  • Slaughterhouse-Five; or, The Children's Crusade: A Duty-Dance with Death
  • The Sirens of Titan
  • Cat's Cradle
